I create the best possible experience for web and software applications based on the goals of the business and the needs of the end user. I’m fascinated by watching people use the products I design and I immediately begin to think of ways to make helpful improvements. Learning is a passion of mine and I often spend my spare time discovering new web technologies and figuring out how I can apply them into my current projects. 

My career started at a small advertising agency project managing every digital project that came through our doors. I left the agency in May of 2014 to pursue a full-time career in user experience for a international software company. This opportunity has given me the chance to work with some of the worlds largest brands such as T-Mobile, Rogers, Vodaphone and ANZ Bank to name a few. 

I care about improving customers experiences by making their online experiences simple and engaging.
