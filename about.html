ABOUT ME

I remember the screeches of my dial up modem and my sister just dying for me to get off the internet so she could use the landline. I remember the sound of “You’ve got mail” and when iFrames were the coolest. While others were busy interrupting my internet connection I was experimenting with the web; creating overly designed pixel perfect Geocities websites and forums about nerdy topics.

Well, I don’t have dial up anymore and it’s been a while since I’ve seen an iFrame but I certainly have not lost my passion for the web. These days you can find me seeking out learning materials on the latest technologies, experimenting with animations and crafting experiences even my not so tech savvy friends can use.

Tools I use

SketchApp, InvisionApp, HTML/CSS/JS (hand coding prototypes), Adobe Creative Cloud, JS Fiddle and Codepen, Pencil and Paper, Paper Prototypes, Usertesting.com, Bootstrap.

Where I work

You can currently find me working at ResponseTek as a User Experience Designer. I work on all parts of the application and being the only UX designer the I preform a diverse variety of UX work.
