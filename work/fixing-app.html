Fixing App

Role: Full Stack Developer

Skills Used: Wireframes, Visual Design, HTML/CSS/JS Development, Ruby on Rails Development

The fixing App was an idea I came up with just before I started studying at CodeCore Bootcamp. Prior to my professional working experience in the advertising industry, I was working in a guitar store doing repairs. The purpose of my application was to modernize the way in which we keep track of customer repairs for retail stores.

The interface I designed for the web application was created in a way that would allow future versions of the web App to translate easier to a tablet or mobile application. My plans for the future is to create an App customers can use with their smartphone or tablet so they can easily take pictures of the item being repaired.
