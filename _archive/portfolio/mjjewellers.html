MJ Jewellers Website

Role: User Experience Designer and Project Manager.

Team Members: Jake Hope (Designer), Andrew Sharpe (Brand Strategy).

About MJ Jewellers: MJ Jewellers is a high end fashion retail store in Vancouver BC. They sell custom made jewellery with only the highest quality materials.

My role began with planning for the content that was going to be on the website along with how it should be presented.

I like starting with sketches to get all of my ideas down on paper. It makes the design and development process much easier and it helps all stakeholders involved determine what is needed.
