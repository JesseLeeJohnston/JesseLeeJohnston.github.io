ResponseTek Referrals

The referral program was a new initiative by ResponseTek that was the idea of our CEO. Traditionally CEM companies and programs focus on turning the detractor into a happy customer but nothing was being done with the people that were deemed promoters of the brand. We're changing that with the referral program by offering the brand advocates rewards for referring us to their friends.  

Role: User Experience Designer
Responsibilities: Wireframes, User Testing, Visual Design, HTML/CSS/JS Development  

This project is one that was developed in a truly agile format. We worked with T-Mobile on our pilot launch. Their primary method of getting feedback is via SMS messages to their customers therefore our focus on the experience had to be 100% mobile. The program works on screens of all sizes however we focused our testing efforts primarily on those mobile users.

This project gave me the opportunity to focus on conversions, something I don't often get to do when working on enterprise applications. I tested different iterations making adjustments upon hearing real user feedback. As this project is still fairly new and currently still being worked on at the time of writing this I can only make assumptions about increases in engagement. I will make future modifications once I receive updated conversion data.
