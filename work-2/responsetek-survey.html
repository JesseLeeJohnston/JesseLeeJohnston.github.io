ResponseTek Surveys

Getting customer feedback is the heart of what ResponseTek does. Companies commission ResponseTek to send out surveys on their behalf to gain a better understanding of how to improve their operations. Big and small companies all over the world use the product to have a dialogue with their customers.  

Role: User Experience Designer
Responsibilities: Wireframes, User Testing, Visual Design, HTML/CSS/JS Development

The sample of survey I'm showing here uses the ResponseTek brand blue however we designed the surveys so that the blue could be replaced with any companies primary brand colour.

Another important aspect which had to be thought of throughout the design process was how to make sure everything works on various screen sizes. I designed working my way up from mobile to large desktop monitors and not losing anything on any of the screens.

Keeping the surveys engaging is extremely important to maintaining a higher response rate. I wanted to make clicking the buttons feel "fun" so that it would keep peoples attention as they're filling out the survey.  

I’m Jesse Johnston,  a user experience designer from Vancouver, BC. I focus on designing for software as a service web applications. I’m not limited to Vancouver I’m available for freelance opportunities anywhere.
